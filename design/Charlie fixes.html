— What's inside

A tidy home for every half-formed thought.

Capture, route, and close the loop. No more "did we ever ship that?" in standup.

①

Capture in one keystroke

Press ⌘K from anywhere. Screenshot, voice note, or paragraph — it lands in the right queue automatically.

②

Route without asking

Relaybin reads the context and suggests an owner. Wrong one? Tap a face and it learns.

③

Close the loop loudly

When something ships, everyone who asked gets a personal little confetti moment. Worth it.

④

Retro on autopilot

Your weekly summary writes itself from the queue. Edit, don't author.

Slack, Linear, GitHub, Notion. Two-way sync, no weird webhooks.

⑥

Private by default

SOC 2, SSO, audit logs. Data stays in your region. Obviously.
